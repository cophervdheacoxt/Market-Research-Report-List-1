<p><strong>The Cloud Video Conferencing Solutions Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Scalable Video Coding (SVC) Type</li><li>Advanced Video Coding (AVC) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cloud Video Conferencing Solutions market is segmented into Scalable Video Coding (SVC) and Advanced Video Coding (AVC) types. SVC allows for adaptive streaming by encoding video at multiple resolutions, enabling better quality for varying bandwidths and devices. This makes it ideal for large-scale conferencing and diverse user networks. In contrast, AVC, a widely adopted compression algorithm, optimizes video quality at a specific bitrate but does not adapt as dynamically. Both types cater to different user needs in video conferencing environments.</p></p>
